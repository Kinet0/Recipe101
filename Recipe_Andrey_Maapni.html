RECIPE

  • 1 kg of Chicken Breasts
  • Soy Sauce
  • One head of Garlic
  • Crushed Pepper
  • Bay Leaves
  • 2 medium size onion
  • 1/4 cup of oil
  • 2 cups of water
  • onions

METHOD

  1. chop ingredients accordingly
  2. wash ingredients
  3. boil water on pan
  4. put chopped 1kg chicken inside the pot
  5. put chopped garlic and onions
  6. put the right amount of soy sauce
  7. put right amount of pepper
  8. put bay leaves
  9. cover the pan and wait for 45 mins
  10. if the water has simmered put oil then saute
  11. after saute remove from heat then serve
  12. voila, you have Chicken Adobo
